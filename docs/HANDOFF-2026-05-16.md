## 2. Product Positioning

Deeper China is an English-first China travel information AI distribution and conversion platform for high-value foreign cultural travelers.

It is not a generic China travel guide, not a Chinese mass-market travel portal, and not a "Top 10 attractions" SEO site.

Core product loop:

```text
Inspire -> Decide -> Route -> Leave contact / book consultation / get expert introduction
```

Primary product surfaces:

```text
/                      Homepage
/destinations          Destination selection and route combinations
/destinations/[slug]   Deep city guide pages
/chat                  Text-first concierge and conversion surface
/experts               Managed expert introduction page
/booking               Booking / consultation flow
/account               Light account/workspace placeholder
```

Brand and writing principles:

```text
English-first
Refined
Culture-led
Editorial
Quiet luxury
Text-first concierge
Founder-operated marketplace MVP
```

Do not change the product into:

```text
Generic travel portal
Chinese-language mass guide
OTA-style deal site
Random AI chatbot
Realtime voice-first product
Large RAG/embedding experiment before content quality is solid
```

```text

```

Current `/api/chat` behavior:

```text
Validates message/history with Zod.
Calls runConciergeTurn.
Streams SSE: meta, line chunks, done.
```

Current concierge behavior:

```text
Infers interest, preferred cities, audience, category, pace, days, budget, depth.
Fetches Supabase knowledge when configured.
Falls back to static JSONL knowledge when Supabase is unavailable.
Uses route combination intent detection for multi-city questions.
Uses duration matching so 10-day first-China queries prefer 9-12 day routes.
Defers expert evidence for generic multi-city planning questions.
```

Verified test query:

```text

```

Expected result:

```text

summary uses First China cultural arc
expert evidence deferred
```

Current desired answer direction:

```text
Beijing -> Chengdu -> Chongqing

Beijing gives historical grammar.
Chengdu softens the trip with food, pandas, tea, parks.
Chongqing finishes with 8D visual intensity, nightscape, hotpot.
```
